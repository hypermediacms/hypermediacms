  - amount: "1/2"
    unit: cup
    item: tahini
  - amount: "1/4"
    unit: cup
    item: lemon juice
  - amount: "2"
    unit: cloves
    item: garlic
  - amount: "1/2"
    unit: tsp
    item: cumin
  - amount: "1/2"
    unit: tsp
    item: salt
  - amount: "2-4"
    unit: tbsp
    item: reserved chickpea liquid
  - amount: "2"
    unit: tbsp
    item: olive oil
  - amount: ""
    unit: ""
    item: paprika for garnish
steps:
  - instruction: Add tahini and lemon juice to a food processor. Blend 1 minute until creamy.
    tip: Processing the tahini first makes smoother hummus.
  - instruction: Add garlic, cumin, and salt. Process 30 seconds.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add chickpeas. Process 1-2 minutes, scraping down sides.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: With processor running, drizzle in reserved liquid until desired consistency.
    tip: More liquid = creamier hummus.
  - instruction: Taste and adjust salt and lemon.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Transfer to a serving bowl. Create a swirl with the back of a spoon.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with paprika.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Serve with pita bread, vegetables, or crackers.
    tip: Keeps refrigerated up to 1 week.
