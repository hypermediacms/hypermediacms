  - amount: "1"
    unit: lb
    item: spaghetti
  - amount: "6"
    unit: tbsp
    item: butter
  - amount: "8"
    unit: cloves
    item: garlic, minced
  - amount: "1/4"
    unit: cup
    item: fresh parsley, chopped
  - amount: "1/2"
    unit: cup
    item: parmesan cheese, grated
  - amount: "1/4"
    unit: tsp
    item: red pepper flakes
  - amount: ""
    unit: ""
    item: salt and pepper to taste
steps:
  - instruction: Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1 cup pasta water before draining.
    tip: Salted water should taste like the sea.
  - instruction: While pasta cooks, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
    tip: Don't let the garlic brown or it will turn bitter.
  - instruction: Add drained pasta to the skillet. Toss to coat with garlic butter.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add pasta water a little at a time if needed to loosen the sauce.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Remove from heat. Stir in parsley and parmesan.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
    tip: Extra parmesan on top is always welcome.
