  - amount: "3/4"

steps:
  - instruction: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    tip: Use an oven thermometer for accuracy.
  - instruction: Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Beat butter with both sugars until light and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.
    tip: Room temperature butter works best.
  - instruction: Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Gradually stir in flour mixture until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
    tip: Do not overmix or cookies will be tough.
  - instruction: Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheets, spacing 2 inches apart.
    tip: Use a cookie scoop for uniform cookies.
  - instruction: Bake 9-11 minutes until edges are golden but centers still look slightly underdone.
    tip: They will continue to cook on the hot pan.
  - instruction: Cool on baking sheet 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack.
