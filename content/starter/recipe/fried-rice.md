steps:
  - instruction: Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add beaten eggs. Scramble until just set. Remove and set aside.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add remaining oil to the wok.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add peas, carrots, and white parts of green onions. Stir fry 2 minutes.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add garlic. Cook 30 seconds.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add cold rice. Break up clumps and stir fry 3-4 minutes until heated through.
    tip: Cold rice fries better than fresh - less mushy.
  - instruction: Add soy sauce and sesame oil. Toss to coat.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Return eggs to pan. Add green onion tops. Toss and serve.
