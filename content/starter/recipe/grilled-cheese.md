steps:
  - instruction: Spread butter on the outside of both bread slices.
    tip: Softened butter spreads easier and more evenly.
  - instruction: Spread mayonnaise on the inside of both slices.
    tip: This is the secret to extra crispy bread.
  - instruction: Layer cheeses on one slice.
    tip: Using two types of cheese = more complex flavor.
  - instruction: Top with second slice, butter side out.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Heat a skillet over medium-low heat.
    tip: Low and slow is the key.
  - instruction: Cook sandwich 3-4 minutes per side until golden and cheese is melted.
    tip: Press gently with a spatula.
  - instruction: Let rest 1 minute before cutting.
    tip: This lets the cheese set slightly.
