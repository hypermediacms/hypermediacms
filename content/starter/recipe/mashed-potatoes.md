title: Creamy Mashed Potatoes
slug: mashed-potatoes
status: published
prep_time: "15"
cook_time: "20"
servings: "6"
ingredients:
  - amount: "3"
    unit: lbs
    item: Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
  - amount: "1/2"
    unit: cup
    item: butter
  - amount: "1"
    unit: cup
    item: heavy cream, warmed
  - amount: "1"
    unit: tsp
    item: salt
  - amount: "1/2"
    unit: tsp
    item: white pepper
  - amount: "2"
    unit: cloves
    item: garlic, minced (optional)
steps:
  - instruction: Place potatoes in a large pot. Cover with cold salted water.
    tip: Starting in cold water ensures even cooking.
  - instruction: Bring to a boil. Cook until fork tender, about 15-20 minutes.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Drain well and return to pot over low heat.
    tip: This dries them out for fluffier mash.
  - instruction: Add butter and mash until mostly smooth.
    tip: Use a potato masher or ricer - never a food processor.
  - instruction: Gradually add warm cream, mixing after each addition.
    tip: Warm cream keeps potatoes fluffy.
  - instruction: Season with salt and pepper.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Add garlic if using. Mix well.
    tip: ""
  - instruction: Serve immediately with extra butter on top.
    tip: Can be kept warm in a slow cooker.

Silky, buttery, and absolutely heavenly. The ultimate comfort side dish.
